---
name: invalid-warranty
description: Response instructions for expired or invalid warranty inquiries
trigger: invalid-warranty
version: 1.0.0
---

<objective>
Generate an empathetic, solution-oriented response explaining that the warranty has expired or is invalid, while offering alternative support options.
</objective>

<response-tone>
- Empathetic and understanding
- Solution-oriented (not just delivering bad news)
- Professional and respectful
- Helpful in providing alternatives
</response-tone>

<required-information>
Include in response:
- Greeting and acknowledgment of their inquiry
- Clear explanation that warranty has expired or is invalid
- Warranty expiration date (if applicable)
- Empathetic acknowledgment of their situation
- Alternative options (extended warranty, paid repair services, upgrade options)
- Contact information for paid support
- Professional closing
</required-information>

<response-structure>
1. Greeting and acknowledgment
2. Warranty status explanation (expired/invalid with date if available)
3. Empathetic acknowledgment
4. Alternative options and solutions
5. Next steps if they want to proceed with alternatives
6. Support contact information
7. Professional closing
</response-structure>

<examples>
Example of a good invalid/expired warranty response:

"Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out regarding your warranty inquiry for serial number SN12345.

I've checked our records, and I see that your warranty expired on June 30, 2024. While your original warranty period has concluded, I understand this may be disappointing, and I'd like to help you explore your options.

We offer several alternatives to ensure your product continues to serve you well:
1. Extended Warranty Plans: Extend your coverage with our comprehensive service plans
2. Paid Repair Services: Our certified technicians can diagnose and repair your product
3. Upgrade Options: Consider upgrading to our latest models with new warranty coverage

To discuss which option works best for you, please contact our customer support team at support@example.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT. They'll be happy to provide pricing and help you choose the right solution.

We appreciate your business and look forward to continuing to support you.

Best regards,
Customer Support Team"
</examples>

<avoid>
- Do not be abrupt or dismissive about expired warranty
- Do not apologize excessively (it's standard business practice)
- Do not make false promises about free service
- Do not suggest workarounds to warranty expiration
- Do not pressure customers into purchasing extended warranties
</avoid>
